MASUGCON WATER FILTER

THE 3 COMPONENTS OF WATER FILTER

The MSC (MaSugCon) water filter is designed to provide safe drinking water to communities as well as households. By utilizing the three components (Malunggay seeds, sugarcane, and coconut husk), this filter eliminates contaminants and adds beneficial minerals, securing the health and well-being of the customers. The filter uses a three-stage filtration process, including sediment filtration and activated carbon filtration using the three components.The compact design of the filter allows for easy and fast installation and maintenance of the water filter, making it suitable for households.
